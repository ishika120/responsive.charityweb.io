About Us

Small effort make a big difference


“No one has ever become poor by giving” is a famous quote by Anne Frank that captures the heart of many people. Charity is a major mood-booster that implicitly invigorates the donors’ lives. Its benefits even extend far beyond tax reductions and recognitions. Accordingly, studies reveal that people who engage in charities feel more fulfilled and happier than without. Though charities cover a broad range of missions, they all have a similar objective – to promote social welfare to the less fortunate.
